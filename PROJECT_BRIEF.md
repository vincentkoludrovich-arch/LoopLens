# LoopLens

LoopLens is an AI-powered recycling intelligence platform for campuses, apartment buildings, offices, and event venues.

## One-line pitch

LoopLens helps organizations reduce recycling contamination and recover more reusable material by combining AI waste recognition, local disposal guidance, and impact analytics.

## Why this can matter

Recycling systems fail when people are confused, contamination spreads, and facilities teams only discover problems after materials are rejected or landfilled. LoopLens creates a feedback loop before that happens.

## Core product

1. Photo-based contamination detection for bins and waste stations.
2. Item-level AI guidance for students, tenants, and employees.
3. Location-aware sorting rules instead of generic advice.
4. A dashboard showing diversion improvement, landfill avoided, and cost savings.

## MVP

Build a web app that lets a user:

1. Upload or simulate a waste image.
2. Detect likely contamination or sorting issues.
3. Get a plain-language recommendation.
4. See an estimated impact dashboard for that location.

## Current product status

The current product includes:

1. A more product-like analysis workspace.
2. Image upload preview for simulated computer vision intake.
3. Scenario controls for location, material, residue, and waste volume.
4. An auto-generated impact report with operational recommendations.
5. A simple forecast dashboard for diversion lift, landfill avoided, and savings.
